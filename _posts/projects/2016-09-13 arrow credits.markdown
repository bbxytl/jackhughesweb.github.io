Arrow was made by [Jack Hughes](http://jackhughesweb.com) as a way to read [Quiver](https://github.com/HappenApps/Quiver/wiki/Quiver-Data-Format) libraries and notebooks on iOS devices.

The most recent version of this app uses SwiftyDropbox, LTMarkdownParser, MMMarkdown, Regex, ReachabilitySwift, MBProgressHUD.
For older versions, view the history of this page on GitHub.
